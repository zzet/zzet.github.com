## Data Collection

This site uses **Google Analytics (GA4)** to analyse traffic. Analytics helps understand which articles are useful and guides improvements to the site's content.

Google Analytics collects anonymised data:

- pages you visit
- time spent on the site
- country and device type (no precise location)
- traffic source (search engine, direct, referral, etc.)

**No personal data (name, email, IP address) is collected or stored on this site.**

## Cookies and Consent

We use [Google Consent Mode v2](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/9976101). This means:

- **Before your consent** — Google Analytics operates in a limited mode without identification cookies.
- **After accepting** — Analytics can set cookies for more accurate measurement.
- **After declining** — no analytics cookies are set.

Your choice is stored in your browser's `localStorage`. You can change it at any time by clearing site data in your browser settings.

## Sharing Data with Third Parties

Analytics data is processed by Google LLC in accordance with their [privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not sell, trade, or share visitor data with any other third parties.

## Advertising

This site carries **no advertising**. No ad networks have access to visitor data.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- request information about stored data
- request its deletion
- withdraw consent for analytics

To exercise these rights, contact **me@zzet.org**.

## Policy Changes

When significant changes are made, the "Updated" date at the top of this page will be updated. We recommend checking this page periodically.
